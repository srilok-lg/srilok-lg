Carousel Ad Unit

Carousel Ad Unit
webOS webOS 4.5 & newer
TV Model 2019 & newer
FILE FORMAT PNG/JPG for Image, MP4 for Video
DIMENSIONS The Carousel ad unit is a 1629×420 canvas with the option to display an Image, Video or an Image with Video Overlay (Companion).
• Image: 1629×420
• Video: 1629×420 (1628×420 also accepted).
• Image with Video Overlay: Image is 1629×420. Video is 16:9 ratio, can also accept 1.85:1 (cinematic).
AVAILABLE INTERACTION Click to App
Click to Deeplink
Click to Web
Click to Video
Click to Image
Click to Custom Landing Page
Reference Ad Interactions for further details.
SPECIFICATION Max File Size: 3MB for Image; 30MB for Video
Video Duration: :6s or :15s to :30s; Video Codec: h264
Audio Codec: AAC preferred, PCM not supported
NOTES • The ad unit displays up to 5 cards and will auto rotate as long as the user remains on the screen. The rotation will pause upon user interaction.
• Videos are clickable and play automatically with no sound. Sound will play when user hovers over video.
• Include an end card within the video to avoid a black screen as this is the final frame and will remain on screen for :5s
• For Image with Video Overlay (Companion), place key content in the right half of the Image as the Video Overlay is displayed on the left hand side.
• Please use the accompanying design template to create the ad so that the right area is in view.
TRACKING 1st party, 3rd party with 1×1 pixel (without action/redirect)
INTERNAL NOTES The Creative Approval Process is REQUIRED for all General Market Advertisers for Carousel Ad Units regardless of format type.

Home Launcher Ad Unit

Home Launcher Ad Unit
webOS webOS 4.5 & 5.0
TV Model 2019, 2020
Home Screen Halo Home Screen
FILE FORMAT PNG/JPG/GIF
DIMENSIONS 300×250
AVAILABLE INTERACTION Click to App
Click to Deeplink
Click to Web
Click to Video
Click to Image
Click to Overlay
Reference Ad Interactions for further details.
SPECIFICATION Max File Size: 150KB
GIF Frame Rate: Between :0.2 and :5s
GIF Number of Frames: Up to 4
GIF Duration: Less than or equal to :15s
NOTES Avoid small fonts and include a click interaction message for animated GIFs.
TRACKING 1st party, 3rd party with 1×1 pixel (without action/redirect)
RESTRICTIONS In Germany, Click to Video, Click to Image & Click to Overlay ad interactions are not allowed for the Home Launcher ad unit, on the Halo Home Screen.

The Halo Home Screen is available on a limited number of 2019 & 2020 TV Sets that have not yet upgraded to the Carousel Home Screen.
