User segments required for targeting are found on the Segment Portal.

These include LG Native (TLAMP), ACR, Demographic, 3rd party(Liveramp, Experian) etc.

All these details get fed into the targeting section of the placement tab in the Mosaic campaign page. Segments are identified by their unique Segment ID.
