DSP

Confluence Page

A Demand-Side Platform (DSP) in ad tech automates digital ad buying across multiple exchanges. It enables real-time bidding on ad impressions, offers precise audience targeting based on factors like demographics and behavior, and integrates with data management platforms to enhance targeting using 1st party and 3rd party data.

Peregrine is our in-house DSP which runs only the Native ads. All other ad types are run on a 3rd party DSP called Beeswax
